About Us
Straw Hat Studio

Straw Hat Studio is a professional photography & videography service provider specializing in telling stories and creative photography. We offer a wide range of services including family portraits, senior photos, maternity sessions, and business headshots. With a passion for capturing genuine moments and a keen eye for detail, we provide beautiful, high-quality images that tell a unique story. We promise to be friendly and provide professional service, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all our clients.
