F.A.Qs
Session Questions

We understand that you may have many questions about our photography and videography services, here are some answers for your questions!

Have more or specific questions, book a call or make it simple by

strawhatstudio@outlook.com
  • Can I book an appointment before a day?
    It’s generally best to book an appointment in advance to ensure availability, but if you need to schedule something for the next day, please contact us directly, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your request.
  • What rights do I have to the photos?
    You will have full usage rights for the photos, allowing you to use them for your intended purposes such as marketing or personal use.
  • Will you help with posing?
    Yes, absolutely! We provide guidance and direction to help you and your guests feel comfortable and look great in your photos. Whether you need help with specific poses or general tips for natural-looking shots, we're here to assist throughout the session.
  • Can we request specific shots?
    Absolutely! You can request specific shots for your photography videography session. Just let us know what you're looking for, and we'll work to incorporate those into your shoot.
  • What should I wear for the portrait session? Am I allowed to bring multiple outfits?
    For your portrait session, wear outfits that make you feel comfortable and confident. Solid colors or subtle patterns work best, as they keep the focus on you rather than your clothing. Feel free to bring multiple outfits if you'd like; just let us know in advance so we can allocate enough time for changes and select a specific location based on your outfit.
  • Can I suggest a location for the shoot?
    Yes, absolutely! We welcome and encourage clients to suggest locations for the shoot. If you have a specific spot in mind, let us know, and we’ll work with you to ensure it’s suitable for your vision.
